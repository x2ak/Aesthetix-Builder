bespoke websites and booking systems exclusively

===============================================================
DESIGN SYSTEM
===============================================================

PALETTE:
--cream: #F7F4EE
--surface: #FFFFFF
--charcoal: #1A1A1C
--charcoal-soft: #2E2E32
--ink-soft: #4A4A4E
--ink-mute: #8A8A8E
--line: #E5DFD3
--gold: #C9A961
--gold-hover: #A8894A
--gold-tint: #F5EDD9
--sage: #6B8E5A
--blush: #E8D5D2

NEVER use pure white (#FFFFFF) as a background.
Cream #F7F4EE is the base background everywhere.
Gold is surgical — hairlines, icons, accents, 
one key CTA treatment. Never flood with gold.
Blue does not exist in this palette.

TYPOGRAPHY:
Display/headlines: Instrument Serif (italic moments)
Body/UI: Inter Tight or Geist

Import Instrument Serif from Google Fonts.
Use Inter Tight as system fallback.

Key typographic moments:
  Italic Instrument Serif in gold on one or two 
  words in each major headline — this is the 
  signature of the brand. Never overdo it.

CORNERS: 10–16px border-radius throughout
SHADOWS: rgba(26,26,28,0.06) — warm, never harsh black
SPACING: generous — white space is premium here

MOTION RULES (Framer Motion):
  whileInView fade + slideUp on every section
  stagger children with 0.1s delays
  Buttons: hover scale 1.02, active scale 0.98
  Links: gold underline grows from left on hover
  Hero animations start on load with delays
  Gold accents feel earned — never spammed
